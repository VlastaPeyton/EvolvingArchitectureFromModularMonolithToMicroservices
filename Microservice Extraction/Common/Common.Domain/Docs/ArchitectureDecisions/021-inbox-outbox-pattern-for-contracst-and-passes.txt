Datum:
	25.8.2025

Problem:
	Robustna async komunikacija izmedju Contracts and Passes za sada nije moguca.

Decision:
	Inbox-Outbox pattern uvodim.

Consequences:
	Dodaje complexnost u komunikaciji izmedju Contracts i Passes. 
	Reduces risk of processing same message(event) twice.
	Introduce a slight performance overhead due to the additional processing and storage requirements associated with managing two distinct message queues.
